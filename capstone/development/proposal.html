Proposal

My capstone project will be an interactive, anonymous forum for sharing and reading the methods of others in their process of accepting and loving their bodies. The target audience is women and femmes ages 18-30 and the lens discussed in class most related to this idea is probably "Challenging Mental Models."

The basic idea is for participants to type in their thought based on the prompt given to them on a relatively blank or plain page, and then as soon as they hit enter, flower petals appear, each containing the answers of past participants. Hovering the mouse over the petals would rustle them on the screen and reveal other petals below, and they'd be able to drag and drop them to move them around and click on them to bring them closer to read.

One concern is risk of spam or entries that are off-topic, innapropriate, too sensitive, or revealing too much private info of the user. I could help prevent this by including some guidelines or monitoring by keywords somehow, perhaps with a library.
